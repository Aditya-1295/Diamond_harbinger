ABOUT US

Diamond Harbinger simplifies the way people shop. We are an independent firm that helps them select diamonds.
We understand how stressful it can be to shop for a diamond, and we aim to simplify the process by providing a place to compare diamonds by carat, color, cut, clarity, price and other diamond specifications.

Why DiamondHarbinger?

Diamond Harbinger lets you predict the price on the basis of the input(cut ,color ,clarity) you put in there. It makes you aware of the price before actually going to the shop to buy a diamond. It rather helps you by preventing you by getting ripped off by the retailer.
